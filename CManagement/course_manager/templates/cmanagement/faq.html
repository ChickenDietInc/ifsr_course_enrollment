FAQ

Why is everything in english?

We started with english since it does not exclude foreign students from joining the courses. When we find the time, a german version of the site will be offered as well.

Can I accredit my chosen course as studium generale or AQuA?

It depends:

If you are member of the faculty of computer science, you can only get AQuA by beeing a tutor.

Otherwise, studium generale is available for everyone, while AQuA is only recognized by certain faculties. If you are not sure, feel free to ask.
